Avatar.
Avatars are used to give a recognisable visual identity such as profile pictures.
Usually found in circular & squarical shapes & varies across different sizes depending on components.
Standard Avatar
These are basic rounded avatars for common purposes.
Simply add avatar to parent class.
Avatar Sizes
Rounded avatars with varying sizes like Extra large, Medium etc.
Square Avatar
To ensure your image is sqaure, use the avatar-square class.
Text Avatars
Get a text based avatar by adding avatar-text.
